## Getting Started
This project will be built using Claude Code Web.

## Vision
GolfHQ.ai is the operating system for golf - your AI caddy Riley B guides you through every aspect of your golf journey. From booking tee times to tracking rounds, reviewing courses, competing in leagues, and improving your game.

## Core Features
- **Dashboard** - Personalized golf command center
- **My Caddy (Riley B)** - AI-powered lessons, tips, and coaching
- **Golf Journal** - Smart scorecard tracking with trophy balls
- **Course Reviews** - The Letterboxd of golf courses
- **Stymie League** - Community competitions and tournaments
- **Tour Card** - Gamification with earned status levels
- **Pro Shop** - Equipment tracking and instructor marketplace

## User Focus
Built for Jason Adams in Saint George, Utah - an 8 handicap golfer who plays 2-3x per week at Fox Hollow, Black Desert, and Sand Hollow.
