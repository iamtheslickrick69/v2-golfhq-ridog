# v2:GolfHQ-Ridog - Project Blueprint

## Vision Statement
GolfHQ.ai is the AI-powered digital country club that lives in every golfer's pocket. We're building the operating system for golf - where Riley B (Ridog), your AI caddy and PGA pro buddy, guides you through every aspect of your golf journey.

## Core Value Propositions
1. **Personal AI Caddy** - Riley knows your game and helps you improve
2. **Earned Status** - Tour Card levels that money can't buy
3. **Community First** - The Letterboxd of golf courses
4. **Complete Ecosystem** - From booking to learning to competing

## User Persona: Jason Adams
- **Location**: Saint George, Utah
- **Handicap**: 8 (trending down)
- **Plays**: 2-3x per week
- **Home Courses**: Fox Hollow, Black Desert, Sand Hollow
- **Goals**: Break into single digit consistently, join amateur tournaments
- **Pain Points**: Finding good playing partners, tracking improvement, booking prime tee times
- **Tech Savvy**: High - uses apps for everything
